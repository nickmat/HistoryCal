Calendar Description

The French Republican calendar was part of the reforms brought in after the French Revolution, the same reforms that lead to the metric system.

Use Case

The calendar had a short life, the details below being the only recorded uses.

  • France, 24 Nov 1793 to 31 Dec 1805 (Gregorian).
  • Paris, May 1871 (Gregorian) during the "Paris Commune".

Record
yearmonthday nmonth decade dday cday

The Record consists of three regular Fields named year, month, and day. There are four extended Fields: nmonth for the named months, decade for the decade number within the month, dday for the day within the decade and cday for the complementary day number. During the complementary days, the nmonth, decade and dday Fields are invalid. Outside the complementary days the cday Field is invalid.

The Epoch is set at year 1 month 1 day 1 (fr) = day 2375840 (jdn).
