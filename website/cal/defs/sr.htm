Calendar Description

This is still a work in progress and is therefore subject to change.

The calendar presented here starts from King Malcolm II's reign on the j# 25 Mar 1005 to the end of King James VI (I of England) reign on j# 27 Mar 1625.

Use Case

The Scottish Regnal calendar has been used for dating of Scottish government and legal documents throughout the period covered by this calendar.

The dates given in the following table are Julian using the Scottish Hybrid calendar. They are based on the book "Scottish Kings" by Sir Archibald H. Dunbar, Bart.
